# v1.2 UI Overhaul — Known Issues

Issues discovered during Phases 23–30 that we are knowingly shipping with. Tokens are user-locked for v1.2, so contrast misses are documented and tracked rather than fixed.

## Contrast (A11Y-01)

Computed via `src/ui/__tests__/contrast.test.ts` using the OKLCH → linear-sRGB → WCAG 2.1 luminance pipeline. All findings are for the **lightest decorative ink (`--ink-4`)** against background surfaces — `--ink-4` is intended for borders, placeholder shading, and disabled glyph chrome, **not body text**. The body ink (`--ink`, `--ink-2`) clears AA on every surface.

### Light theme
| Foreground | Background | Ratio | Threshold | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `--ink-4` | `--bg` | 2.56:1 | 3.0:1 (UI/large) | Used for rules + placeholder chrome; not body text |
| `--ink-4` | `--bg-2` | 2.45:1 | 3.0:1 | Same |
| `--ink-4` | `--bg-3` | 2.27:1 | 3.0:1 | Same |

### Dark theme
| Foreground | Background | Ratio | Threshold | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `--ink-4` | `--bg-2` | 2.89:1 | 3.0:1 (UI/large) | Borderline; the 2.89 is well within the just-noticeable range of 3.0 |
| `--ink-4` | `--bg-3` | 2.60:1 | 3.0:1 | Same |

**Action:** none in v1.2. Logged in `.planning/v1.2-followup.md` under the A11y bucket. A future minor version can bump `--ink-4` darkness one step in OKLCH space (e.g. light `0.66` → `0.60`, dark `0.50` → `0.54`) to clear AA without changing the visual hierarchy meaningfully.
