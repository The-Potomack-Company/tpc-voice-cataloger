<objective>
Close the audio-upload stranding precondition at the source (SC-1). Today a `db.audioUploadQueue` entry that exhausts its 3 retries (recorded offline, app closed before reconnect, transient RLS/network blip) goes to `status:'failed'` where `drainAudioQueue` never re-scans it (it scans `'pending'` only), nothing writes the outcome back to `items`, and the only escape hatch (`retryFailedUploads`) is reachable solely through an `ItemCard` pill requiring a local Dexie blob ‚ÄĒ unreachable for the cross-device/cleared population. The item, already `queued` in Supabase, eventually hits the AI worker which finds no audio in Dexie or Storage and terminally fails it (the 34-item death spiral, GAP-1..GAP-4 in 42-RESEARCH.md).

Make the upload durable and self-healing: a **bounded** `failed‚Üípending` resweep on app boot + `online` (mirroring the proven Phase-41 attempt-cap pattern, not the unbounded retry storm), and a reconcile edge so an item whose audio reaches `upload_status='uploaded'` becomes drainable again. Resolve RESEARCH Open-Q2 with the **lower-blast-radius re-queue design** (reuse `offlineQueue`'s attempt cap and the existing pending-reclaim union) ‚ÄĒ do NOT gate `ai_status='queued'` at `RecordButton`, and do NOT touch the fire-and-forget recorder hot path.

Purpose: no item can end with a synced `items` row but no recoverable `audio` row.
Output: durable, resumable audio upload queue + audio‚ÜĒitem reconciliation; zero schema changes, zero new deps.
</objective>
